Apple watch(info)

What is Apple Watch? In 2014 Apple watch was presented by the CEO Tim Cook in conjunction with iPhone 6, it was marked as fashion, accessory. It officially released in April 2015 it is also made for more healthy. Apple decided to make a watch and only then set out to discover what it might be good for you “There was a sense that technology was going to move onto the body”, said by Alan Dye, who runs Apple’s human interface group.

What are the latest features of an Apple Watch? There is a massive feature on apple watch called “The digital crown”, which is the rotating button on the side of Apple Watch. Press and hold the Digital Crown to use Siri, turn it to scroll or zoom, press it to view the Watch face or Home Screen, and double-press to open the last used app. Well, that’s only starting, there are so many other cool beneficial attributes and quality present here. One of them is being water resistant. Your Apple Watch is water resistant, not waterproof. Therefore, as an example, you can wear and use your Apple Watch during exercise (exposure to sweat is OK), in the rain, and while washing your hands. Nevertheless, it is not completely waterproof.

Why was Apple Watch created? Apparently, Apple was looking at ways to deliver on Jobs' goal of making their customers healthier by using technology to help monitor and track health related data points. It became clear to them that they would need some type of mobile device platform to do this.

What is the concept of Apple Watch? Apple Watch is a wearable smartwatch that allows users to accomplish a variety of tasks, including making phone calls, sending text messages and reading email.

What are the benefits of Apple Watch? Here are the top ways your Apple Watch can help you get healthier -- and potentially save your life. Warn you of loud noise to protect against hearing damage. ... Track your menstrual cycle. ... Detect falls and call for help. ... Alert you if you have an abnormal heart rhythm. ...
